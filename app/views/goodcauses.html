Didsbury Good Neighbours

The core of our work is befriending isolated older people. We do this by recruiting and training volunteer befrienders; they are then matched with an older person who they visit regularly at home.

St Catherines Social Club

The parish Social Club was conceived over 40 years ago by the wonderful Fr Jack Fay, then parish priest. He envisaged the format to be run by a Committee to contribute to the spiritual and social development of the community.
