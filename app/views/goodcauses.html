Together Dementia Support

Together Dementia Support provides a range of activities with the aim of improving the quality of life of people with dementia and of their carers. We believe that people with this condition still have much to contribute to society and should be enabled to stay physically fit and have fun. We are also keen to enable the voice of people with dementia and their carers to be heard in shaping and campaigning for better services.
