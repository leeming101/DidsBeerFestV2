Didsbury Beer Festival will not take place in 2020!

In view of the continuing situation with Covid-19, and the uncertainty about when restrictions will be relaxed to allow large gatherings, regrettably there will not be a Didsbury Beer Festival in 2020.

Hopefully the recent easing of lockdown conditions will continue and we will be able to support our local pubs through the remainder of this year.
