Collection of DBF at Home Beer Boxes.

As advised when the orders were made, collection of the beer boxes is ONLY available on either 7th November or 14th November between 11am and 4pm from St Catherine’s Community Centre, School Lane, M20 6HS.

The collection point will be outside the building and if there are others arriving at the same time we ask that you respect social distancing and wear a mask. Please be ready to quote your order number and if collecting for someone else please bring a copy of their order acknowledgement email.

Collection is in line with current Government restrictions: “Pre-ordered alcohol can be collected by customers as long as they do not enter the premises. They must order their drink by a website, phone or text message”.
