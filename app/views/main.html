It's here!

At last, here is the opportunity to buy a box for your Didsbury Beer Festival at Home, to drink at a safe distance or perhaps with friends on a zoom meeting.

What’s in the box?


Option1: 2 beers and a glass for £12
•  The special 2020 festival glass (pint only)
•  A Bottle of the traditional festival ale (4%)
•  A special bottle of 4.1% blond ale (Teresa’s Tipple) to celebrate Teresa Seneviratne, who died earlier this year. She was a founder of the festival and involved in the planning of the first one in 2008.




Option 2: 5 beers and a glass for £22
This bigger box includes the above plus 3 extra beers from local breweries:
• Thirst Class - American Pale Ale 5.3%
• Bollington – Oatmeal Stout 5.0%
• Track. – Sonoma 3.8%



How to order

Order online by clicking one of the links below and then collect your order on 7th or 14th November from St Catherine’s Community Centre (the home of the Didsbury Beer Festival).

There is very limited stock so you will need to be quick.

The Didsbury Beer Festival has always supported local good causes. This year all the profit will be supporting those most in need by donating to local food banks - Chorlton and Didsbury Foodbank (https://chorltondidsbury.foodbank.org.uk/). There is also an option to make an additional donation when placing your order.

Sales are strictly for over 18s and proof of age will be required on collection
